Guide to Creating a Track
Track: The Performed Version

A Track represents a specific performance or recorded version of a Work or part of a Work. This includes the individual renditions you might find within an album. To group tracks into a physical or digital format, create a Release entry.

What to Include in a Track

Include the details that are unique to the particular performance or recording, such as the performers, the arrangement, and any specific variations from the original Work. Link it to the Work it's derived from to maintain the connection.
