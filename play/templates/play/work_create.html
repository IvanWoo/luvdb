Guide

You are creating a Work entry of a game.

A Work entry is used for creating better linkage between different releases of the same game. It's not a must.

Examples

The visual novel WHITE ALBUM2 was first released in two parts: introductory chapter and closing chapter, and later was ported to PS3 in one volume. All three are the same games but different releases, so we need a Work entry to group them together.
