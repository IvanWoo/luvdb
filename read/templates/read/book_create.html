Guide

You are creating a Book entry.

A Book is a physical copy in the 3-level model of the represention of books: Work > Instance > Book. This model enable us to create better linkage between different editions of a book, and build comprehensive bibliography for the author.

A Book entry contains more information about the physical copy of the book, such as the ISBN, the publisher, the publication date, etc.

A Book can be linked to one Instances (e.g. a novel), or multiple instances (e.g. a compilation of short stories or essays).

Examples
  • This 2007 paperback edition of The Annotated Prided and Prejudice is a reprint of the 2003 hardcover edition, both are Books of the same Instance.
  • All But Impossible is a short story collection by Edward D. Hoch, which links to 15 Instances of short stories.
