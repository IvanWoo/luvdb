Guide to Creating a Work

Work: The Conceptual Entity

A Work represents the high-level intellectual or artistic concept of a book. It's the abstraction of the idea, irrespective of individual editions or formats. If you're entering specific details about a particular edition, navigate to the Instance section.

What to Include in a Work

Define the Work by its universal attributes such as title, author, and genre. These details remain constant across all Instances and Books related to this Work.
