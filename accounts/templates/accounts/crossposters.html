Manage Your Crossposters

Here, you can link your LuvDB feed to Mastodon and BlueSky, allowing for seamless crossposting of your content. Connect your Mastodon account using your developer tokens and your BlueSky account using your BlueSky app passwords to start sharing your LuvDB activities across platforms. You can only link one account per platform, but you can unlink and change them at any time.
